About me

I'm a UX Designer and Researcher rooted with user centered design thinking to create satisfying experiences. I truly believe in accessible design and my interests lie in designing inclusive products and services. I currently research on Accessibility and Assistive Technologies with the Trace R&D Center. Former UX Designer Intern at Dassault Systems SolidWorks Corp and a Product Analyst at Temenos.

In my free time, I like to explore new places and read fiction novels . I am also an amateur photographer and I love to capture moments that are beautifully perceived by my eyes.

Looking for full-time opportunities starting July 2019!

My Design Process

I start by discovering and uncovering user needs with which I define the problem at hand that needs resolving. Later on, with more user needs and specification I use user-centered design thinking to ideate on design solutions. Finally, I test my designs with users to get their feedback and to check inclusivity with which I make further iterations.
